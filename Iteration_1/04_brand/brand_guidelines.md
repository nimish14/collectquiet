# CollectQuiet — Brand Guidelines

## Name & Tagline
- **Name:** CollectQuiet
- **Tagline:** Get paid without the awkward chase.
- **Alt taglines:** "You earned it. We'll ask." / "No accounting software required."

## Positioning
CollectQuiet is the **anti-awkward invoice reminder** for tradespeople and freelancers who already have an accountant but still chase their own receivables. Not a collections agency. Not QuickBooks. A calm, persistent nudge machine.

## Voice
| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Calm, professional, trades-respectful | Aggressive collections tone |
| "Reminder" / "follow-up" | "Demand" / "final warning" (until step 5) |
| Acknowledge client relationship | Shame the user for wanting payment |
| Short sentences | Corporate AR jargon |

**Sample line:** "Hope you're well. Just a quick note that invoice CQ-1042 was due June 12."

## Color Palette
| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Forest | `#0F3D2E` | Primary brand, buttons, nav |
| Forest Light | `#1A5C45` | Hover, links |
| Mint | `#7EE0B8` | Accent, logo ring |
| Mint Soft | `#E8FAF2` | Backgrounds, badges |
| Cream | `#F6F4EF` | Page background |
| Ink | `#14201A` | Body text |

## Typography
- **Headlines:** DM Sans 600 + Instrument Serif italic for emphasis words
- **Body:** DM Sans 400–500
- **UI:** DM Sans 600 for buttons/labels

## Logo
SVG wordmark + icon: dual arcs (payment cycle) with center dot (invoice settled).
Files: `05_product/collectquiet/public/favicon.svg`, inline in app nav.

## Imagery Direction
- Real trades environments (van, job site, invoice on clipboard) — not stock handshakes
- Screenshots of reminder preview, not generic dashboards
- Evidence quotes on landing page with source links

## Brand Don'ts
- No red "PAST DUE" stamp aesthetic until final-notice step
- No "AI-powered" as lead message — automation is implied
- No comparison attacks on PayNudger/Dueflo by name in marketing
