# Founder Pitch C — ScopeSeal (Freelance Change-Order Gate)

## Wedge
When a client asks for "one small tweak," ScopeSeal generates a signed change order before work continues.

## Target Persona
Freelance designers/devs on fixed-price contracts.

**Evidence:** https://www.terms.law/forum/thread/freelance-contract-scope-creep-payment.html — 20 hours unpaid extra work demanded.

## Product Shape
Browser extension + client approval link; blocks deliverable handoff until CO signed.

## Pricing
$19/mo solo, $49/mo agency.

## GTM
Indie Hackers, r/freelance, agency Twitter.

## Moat
Workflow integration at moment of scope request.

## Risk
PactAlert already in market with contract obligation tracking.
