# Founder Pitch B — ReceiptVault (Missing Invoice / Tax Compliance)

## Wedge
Capture and organize supplier invoices so tax deductions aren't lost at 2 AM in April.

## Target Persona
Founders and freelancers who paid via PayPal/card but lack proper invoices.

**Evidence:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/i-built-invoicegenie-because-i-lost-3-200-to-missing-invoices-and-tax-season-was-a-nightmare-690714484b — *"$3,200 in tax deductions I couldn't claim"*

## Product Shape
Email forwarding + AI extraction → organized invoice vault with tax-ready export.

## Pricing
$15/mo, 180 signups in 6 weeks cited by InvoiceGenie founder.

## GTM
Tax-season content marketing, founder communities.

## Moat
Compliance accuracy, accountant integrations.

## Risk
Seasonal demand spike; InvoiceGenie already validating.
