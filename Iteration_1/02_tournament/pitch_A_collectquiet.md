# Founder Pitch A — CollectQuiet (Invoice Follow-Up for Trades & Freelancers)

## Wedge
**CollectQuiet** automates polite invoice reminders so electricians, plumbers, freelancers, and solo contractors never send another awkward "just checking in" email.

## Target Persona
**Mike, 38, solo electrician** — invoices 8–15 commercial GCs/month via email PDF. Uses an accountant for taxes, not QuickBooks daily. Loses track of who paid. Hates chasing because it feels "desperate."

**Evidence:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/chasing-overdue-invoices-is-awkward-i-built-a-small-tool-to-automate-reminders-4f89bae266

## Product Shape (v1)
- Add invoice in 30 seconds (client, amount, due date, optional payment link)
- Pre-built 5-step escalation: friendly → firm → pause-work warning → final notice
- Sends from user's own email identity (SMTP connect or copy-paste templates in v1 demo)
- Dashboard: overdue aging buckets, reminder log, recovered $ estimate
- Works standalone — no accounting suite required

## Pricing
- **Starter $12/mo** — 25 active invoices, email reminders
- **Pro $24/mo** — unlimited invoices, SMS add-on, payment-link open tracking
- 14-day trial, month-to-month (anti-lock-in vs ServiceTitan/AppFolio pattern)

## GTM (90 days)
1. **Week 1–2:** Launch on Indie Hackers, electrician/HVAC Facebook groups, trade subreddits (organic posts only — no deploy per guardrails; prepare assets)
2. **Week 3–4:** Partner with 3 trade accountants who hear AR pain weekly
3. **Month 2:** SEO content: "invoice follow-up templates for electricians" (InvoiceBlitz/Waco3 keyword adjacency)
4. **Month 3:** Case study: recover $X in 30 days — target 50 paying users

## Moat
- **Tone library** trained on trades language (not corporate collections agency voice)
- **Reminder audit trail** exportable for small claims
- **Accountant referral channel** — complements rather than replaces bookkeeping
