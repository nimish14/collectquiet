# Researcher 4 — Vertical / Niche Forum Pain Dossier

**Researcher:** 4 (Vertical forums)  
**Date compiled:** 2026-07-12  
**Method:** WebSearch + WebFetch on industry forums and vertical-specific complaint pages

---

## Executive Summary

Vertical forums show **concrete, monetizable operational pain** in beauty/salon (no-shows), home services (FSM distrust), and freelance services (scope creep). The most underserved wedge for a one-person build is **invoice collection for skilled trades who don't use QuickBooks** — electricians and solo contractors feel social friction chasing larger commercial clients.
